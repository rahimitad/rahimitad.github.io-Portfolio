About Me

I have 10 years of experiences in delivering enjoyable user experience across tech-startups, agencies, media companies, hospitality and non-profit fields.

Hello!

In present days, I am a UX Engineer, but there is more to my story...

My choice of work has always been revolving in delivering best user experience journey to the people around me.

My early career as a journalist honed my research and communication skills, in Bahasa Indonesia and English. While my subsequent career in non-profit and aviation laid a solid foundation to better my empathy, in high-risk environment such as 12,000 feet up in the air and remote village in West Papua where electricity was non-existent.

I went to get my feet wet in tech by co-founding a F&B delivery start-up in 2015, being one of the 10 promising tech startups empowered by females in Indonesia in December 2015, according to GirlsInTech.

In 2016, I went back to school to learn more about web development and design at NUS graduate school, and now have finally found the calling in UX.

What I do

I have been building solutions across different UX areas: Research, Design, and Development.

UX Research

Empathy is my main tool, aided with User Interviews, A/B testing, Usability Testing, Market Research, and other feasible research methods.

When tasked with creating a digital product, I shall ask if such product is needed by the users in the first place. This is where research comes handy.

I have done UX researches for Gillette, Dulux, FWD, Migraine Buddy, and RantangExpress.

UX Design

Wireframing, Prototyping, and Continuous Iterations.

Sketch and Principle as my weapons of choice, backed up by data from the research or data science team

I have done UX designs for Dulux, FWD, BreakApp, Migraine Buddy, and RantangExpress.
